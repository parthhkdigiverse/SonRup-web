Biotin & Zinc: The Daily Vitality Shield

June 15, 2026 4 Min Read By Sonrup Nutritionists Team

We are constantly seeking external solutions for vibrant hair, strong nails, and glowing skin. But real beauty and cellular health start from the inside out. Your body requires specific coenzymes and trace minerals to fuel structural protein production. Two of the most essential building blocks in this process are **Biotin** and **Zinc**.

Understanding Biotin (Vitamin B7 / Vitamin H)

Biotin, historically known as Vitamin H (for *Haar und Haut*, German for hair and skin), is a water-soluble B-complex vitamin. It serves as an essential coenzyme in fatty acid synthesis and amino acid metabolism.

More specifically, Biotin plays a direct role in the synthesis of **Keratin**—the fibrous structural protein that forms the foundation of your hair shafts and nail beds. Studies have shown that consistent daily Biotin supplementation significantly improves nail thickness and reduces brittleness.

The Synergistic Power of Zinc

While Biotin builds structural proteins, Zinc protects cellular integrity. Zinc is a trace element vital for cell division, tissue repair, and immune response. Inside the hair follicles, Zinc plays an indispensable role in maintaining the structural oil glands that surround the roots, preventing follicular shedding.

When combined, Biotin and Zinc create a powerful metabolic synergy, accelerating cellular turnover and shield against external oxidative stress from pollution or UV rays.

Enhanced with Vitamin C & E

To further amplify this vitality shield, our Biotin gummies are infused with highly bio-available Vitamin C and Vitamin E. Vitamin C acts as a vital precursor for natural collagen synthesis in the skin layer, while Vitamin E protects lipid cell membranes from free radical aging damage.

Designed Sugar-Free for Real Health

Many daily beauty gummies are loaded with sugars that cause glucose spikes, which ironically break down collagen fibers in a process called glycation.
